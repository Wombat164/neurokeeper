The neurokeeper mark is a **simplified brain whose interior carries the standard sync glyph**: the mind,
kept up to date. That is exactly what the engines do, tend a knowledge graph and keep it current. The
outline is one half mirrored across the centre, so the mark is perfectly symmetric; the ink adapts to
its surface while emerald stays constant.

## Colours

One emerald accent, no gradient. Ink is monochrome and follows the surrounding text; emerald is the one
constant so the "current" signal always reads.

| Role | Light | Dark |
|---|---|---|
| Ink | `#222834` | `#e9edf2` |
| Emerald (accent) | `#10b981` | `#25cd94` |
| Paper / ground | `#f6f7f9` | `#0f1319` |

## Usage

**Do**

- Keep clear space around the mark equal to the height of the sync glyph.
- Use the dark variant on dark grounds; the ink may be recoloured to match surrounding text.
- Let the mark scale down to a 16px favicon; it stays legible.

**Don't**

- Recolour, gradient, or rotate the emerald sync glyph; it is the fixed brand signal.
- Add shadows, bevels, or other effects.
- Place the light mark on a busy or low-contrast ground.
